## Welcome!

The 16th International Natural Language Generation Conference
will be held jointly with the 24th Annual Meeting of the Special Interest Group on Discourse and Dialogue (SIGDial 2023).
The events will take place in Prague, September 11-15 2023.

Call for Workshop proposals is open, please check out the [Calls](https://inlg2023.github.io/calls.html) tab for more information.
